About Me and This Website

First off, hello, and welcome to my website. My name is Hunter, and I am currently a student at Eastern Washington University, majoring in Computer Science with a focus on Cyber Security and minoring in Communication. I am passionate about anything related to computers and technology, especially when it comes to software security. I love to make music, watch the ever-expanding vastness of space, and watch anime. Before I had decided to major in computer science, I had previously wanted to go into Astrophysics due to my love of space and physics.

Initially, randomserver.xyz was a small project to help me learn and better understand how to create a website, use HTML and CSS, and set up a web server. Around 2019, I decided that I wanted this server to more than just a small project. Instead, it would be a repository of sorts where I could archive and share different things that I've created, found, like, and so on.
